# Welcome to Your Website Project Space

This workspace keeps the whole website project in one place.

Instead of long email threads and scattered notes, we will use these pages to collect your goals, visual preferences, feedback, deadlines, and approvals.

## How To Use This Space

- Start with the `Project Brief` page
- Then move to `Brand Direction` and `Style Preferences`
- Use `Content Collection` to clarify what materials already exist and what is still missing
- Review progress any time in `Progress Board`
- Leave requests or ideas directly on the relevant pages whenever possible

## What To Expect

- Clear questions with simple answer options
- Dedicated pages for design direction, content, structure, and feedback
- Shared visibility into progress and upcoming milestones
- A cleaner review process with less back-and-forth

## Response Expectations

Please answer as specifically as you can. The more concrete your replies are, the faster I can turn them into a strong first version.

If you are unsure about something, that is fine. Mark your current preference and add context in the notes where needed.

## Recommended First Steps

1. Complete `Project Brief`
2. Complete `Brand Direction`
3. Complete `Style Preferences`
4. Review `Site Structure`
5. Check `Timeline & Milestones`

## Quick Note

This is a working space, not a polished presentation. Some parts may evolve during the project as ideas become clearer.
